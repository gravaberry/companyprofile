Who We Are

Our main service is providing storage into our new covered warehouse of various sizes and at various location with close proximity to port areas. Warehouse are fitted with electric weighbridge and with modern packaging plant for bagging of bulk products

Our location of warehouses are in Belawan, KIM, Paya Pasir and many more upcoming location in North Sumatra, Indonesia. We ensure and help our clients to clear bulk goods from port to avoid demorrage. We have handled more than 5 million tons of assorted commodities per year
